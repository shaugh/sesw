Environmental Services

Our Environmental Services include:

  • Forensic soil investigation and surveying for:

    • Areas of environmental significance
    • Agricultural development
    • Subdivisions
    • Problem areas
    • Failed crops

  • Soil Surveying, Site Investigation and Land Capability Assessment for specific land use:

    • Horticultural or agricultural development, any scale
    • Areas subject to mining, including rehabilitation advice or pre-mining soil assessment
    • Potential sites for disposal of reclaimed municipal wastewater or industrial wastewater
    • Large scale dryland assessment for crop potential
    • Pre-purchase soil & land assessment

  • Contaminated site investigation and remediation planning.

  • Wastewater management, land application disposal plans and associated soil investigations.

  • Soil, surface water, groundwater & plant sampling to the highest degree of precision according to site specific features and requirements, including on-going site monitoring and assessment

  • Environmental Impact Assessments for agribusiness and agricultural development.

  • Site investigation and design options for landfill cells, leachate control barriers, landfill capping and revegetation.

  • Land capability assessments and site suitability assessment for specific land uses, irrigation developments, industrial or municipal development.

  • Environmental investigation, soil management and rehabilitation for mining companies.

  • Pre and post earthworks soil assessments for sites subject to all forms of land-forming.

  • Project management and quality assurance supervision for soil, water, geotechnical and groundwater projects.

  • Forensic investigations for potential legal action.

  • Soil assessment and management for proposed and existing parks, gardens, recreational reserves and areas of crown land.

  • Irrigation & soil management plans for parks, gardens & playing fields.

  • Water quality investigation and assessment.

  • Saline site management for irrigated and dryland agriculture including techniques for soil management and irrigation method for leaching salt.

  • Hydrogeological sampling, groundwater sampling & drilling rig supervision.

  • Property pre-purchase inspections.

  • Water quality investigation and assessment.

  • Soil erosion assessment for soil conservation works
