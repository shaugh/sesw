Christian Bannan
B. Agriculture (Hons), Adv. Dip. Agriculture, ASSSI, CPSS (Pending)
Environmental & Agricultural Soil Scientist, Director

Christian is a passionate and motivated soil scientist with 10 years' experience with field consulting and reporting for environmental science, agricultural science, environmental engineering and civil engineering projects.

Christian has direct experience with soil forensic investigation, soil surveying, geotechnical investigation, land capability assessment, contaminated site investigation, irrigation and wastewater management and soil and chemical test analysis. Christians' key priority is to deliver detailed and scientific recommendations for soil amelioration, management, nutrition for enhancing production or soil performance, based on evidence collected in the field and from precise testing.

Christian specialises in soils of the Murray Darling Basin, working regularly throughout the Riverine Plains and Mallee regions of Victoria and south and western New South Wales. Christian has also undertaken work in southern Queensland, South Australia, Western Australia and more recently overseas in India.

Roger Wrigley. Wrigley-Dillon
B. Engineering, M.Engineering Science, ASSSI, CPEng, MIEAust
Civil Engineer & Soil Scientist

Roger Wrigley is a Chartered Professional Engineer with over 35 years of practical experience as a consulting engineer and environmental advocate. Roger has experience with environmental engineering of facilities for feedlots, piggeries, wastewater treatment and re-use and for municipal sites, factories and farms.

Roger also has experience with the investigation, design and construction supervision of irrigation and drainage schemes including dams, pumping stations and hydrological investigations assessing water storages, flooding and salinization processes. Roger provides representation as an expert witness at VCAT, the County and Supreme Courts.

He is also recognised as an expert by the NSW Land and Environment Court and has given evidence on behalf of the NSW Solicitor General Office and the NSW Office of Water. He is also the author of 14 published books & 60+ scientific papers & reports. Roger has undertaken international work in New Zealand, the USA, Uruguay & Oman
