Municipal & Domestic Services

Our Municipal and Domestic Services include:

  • Land capability assessment for:

    • Wastewater management & monitoring
    • Subdivisions
    • Domestic wastewater disposal

  • Testing of soils for standard land capability assessment.

    • Parameters including EC 1:5, Soil pH (1:5 soil water), Slaking Class and Dispersion Class (ASPAC Accreditation Pending).

  • Soil management for parks, gardens, recreational reserves, playing fields and domestic facilities.
