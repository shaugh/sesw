Agricultural Services

We provide forensic soil investigation and surveying for agricultural development and productivity, for operations of all scales including intensive irrigation and horticulture through to low input dryland farming systems.

Our Agricultural Services include:

  • Comprehensive soil chemical and physical analysis including all forms of macro-nutrient and trace element testing, provision of soil chemical and physical amelioration options along with soil and crop nutrition requirements.

  • Property scale soil amelioration plans according to the existing soil constraints. Plans include the following aspects with machinery or specialist equipment recommendations for:

    • Soil Physical Amelioration: Ripping, aeration, clay spreading, mixing, discing, cultivation and stabilisation using organic ameliorants.
    • Soil Chemical Amelioration: Treatment for dispersive soils and acidic soils

  • Plant sampling, analysis and recommendations for foliar fertilizer application and following season nutrient planning.

  • Soil investigation for soil conservation works, dam siting, dam engineering, catchment hydrological investigation and spillway design for farm dams.

  • Saline site management for irrigated and dryland agriculture and techniques for leaching salt.

  • Agricultural chemical residue testing.

  • Irrigation and drainage management planning on irrigated properties.

  • Forensic investigations for potential legal action.

  • Effluent, manure and wastewater management plans and land application requirements for dairy farms, piggeries, feedlots and other intensive industries.

  • Soil suitability assessment and approval for rice growing.

  • Group facilitation, soil pit presentations or classroom presentation for soil management.

  • Water quality investigation and assessment.

  • Property pre-purchase investigations.
