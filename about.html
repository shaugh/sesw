South East Soil & Water
Independent. Regional. Local.

South East Soil & Water are an independent soil and water consultancy located in central Victoria. We are devoted to delivering the highest level of identification and scientific understanding of soil and water parameters to the environmental, agricultural, industrial and domestic industries according to world class best practice, state guidelines and codes of practice.

Passionate. Innovative. Resourceful.

Our staff and associates are passionate, innovative and strive to provide the highest level of analysis and data collection at any site under investigation or assessment, as well as identify new techniques and options for our clients in order to improve decision making, production and business performance for environmental, agricultural, municipal and industrial sectors.

Research. Corporate. Mining. Business. Individual.

We provide consulting services to shire councils, corporate agricultural firms, water authorities, mining companies, agricultural research groups, individuals or any business that requires specialist soil and water services. We draw upon associates with the correct experience in specialist scientific areas to meet your needs. Being a small firm we make this commitment to our clients with lower project delivery costs in comparison to larger firms with higher overheads.

South-Eastern Australia. Murray Darling Basin. National Reach.
